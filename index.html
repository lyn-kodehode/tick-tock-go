How to Play

  1. This is a single-player game.
  2. You have 30 seconds to get three of the same symbols in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.)
  3. Timer starts soon as you make your first move.
  4. A random symbol between {X, O, ♤, ♧, ♡, ♢, ☆, ☾, ☀} will be generated on each box you click.
  5. In every half a second, a random move you made will be deleted which you can re-click to generate a new random symbol.
  6. Click the START button to load the game.
  7. Don't forget to have fun and goodluck!
